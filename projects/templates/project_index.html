About Me

Alex Baowend Sôôm Marie Anicet ZONGO comes from Burkina Faso. Born in Bobo Dioulasso city, he grew up in Ouagadougou city, the capital. Alex defined himself as a disciplined, focused and responsible engineering student. His passion and dedication to sciences come from deep in his childhood during junior High-School where he was mesmerized by the elegance and sophistication of mathematical sciences and their applications.
